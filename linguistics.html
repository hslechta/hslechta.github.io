Reading and Speaking Proficiency: 1 to 10 continuous scale (with explanations below)

Reading Proficiency Key

  • 1 = I can read a few words and recognize the alphabet
  • 3/4 = I can read some sentences, but I need to use a dictionary for most words
  • 6/7 = I can read most sentences, but I need a dictionary for some words
  • 10 = I rarely need to look up a word's meaning

Speaking Proficiency Key

  • 1 = I can pronounce words that I am seeing for the first time
  • 3/4 = I can produce simple sentences and participate in simple conversations
  • 6/7 = I can produce complex sentences and participate in most conversations
  • 10 = I am fluent, and I can communicate any idea I want to communicate
