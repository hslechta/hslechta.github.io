Instrument Details

Voice

I sang Bass II with the Cornell University Glee Club and the Cornell University Chamber Singers.

Cello

I have played cello with the Reading Pops Orchestra.

Percussion

I played an assortment of mallet percussion in high school and college wind ensembles and percussion ensembles.

I also played the Cornell Chimes in college. The instrument is a 21–bell chime in the top of McGraw Tower, played three times a day by student Chimesmasters.

Trombone

I learned to play trombone as part of my high school jazz band.

Keyboards

After learning mallet percussion, I began teaching myself to play piano.

I also began learning organ in a college class during my senior year.

About the Music

The music you're currently hearing is a recording of me playing the hymn tune Slane, better known as Be Thou My Vision, on the Cornell Chimes. If you cannot hear it, you may download it as an mp3 or as an ogg.
